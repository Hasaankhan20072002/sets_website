Unmanned Aerial Platforms & Systems Centre

"Advancing Autonomy, Resilience, and Mission Effectiveness in Unmanned Systems"

Back to SETS

Vision & Mission

Vision

To lead the innovation of autonomous and resilient unmanned aerial platforms that transform defence, security, and civilian missions worldwide.

Mission

To research, prototype, and demonstrate advanced unmanned aerial platforms and systems that combine autonomy, survivability, and mission adaptability across diverse operational domains.

Our Role

The Unmanned Aerial Platforms & Systems Centre (UAPSC) serves as the national hub for research, innovation, and system-level integration of unmanned aerial platforms, ranging from micro-UAS to long-endurance, high-altitude systems.

It leads the design, prototyping, testing, autonomy development, communications integration, and mission-specific payload engineering of unmanned platforms for defence, disaster response, environmental monitoring, logistics, and emerging commercial applications.

The Centre works in close collaboration with super & hypersonics, communications, cybersecurity, and AI Centres to enable next-generation autonomous, resilient, and mission-configurable UAV ecosystems.

Core Tasks

Specialized research and development areas driving UAPSC's mission forward

1

Platform Design, Aerostructures & Airframe Development

  • Develop novel airframe architectures (fixed-wing, rotary-wing, hybrid VTOL, HALE, MALE, micro-UAVs).
  • Perform aerodynamic modeling, CFD analysis, loads analysis, and structural optimization.
  • Research lightweight materials, composites, and modular, reconfigurable structures.
  • Collaborate with super & hypersonics for propulsion compatibility and high-speed envelope considerations.
2

Propulsion System Integration

  • Integrate electric, hybrid, internal combustion, battery, hydrogen, and solar propulsion systems.
  • Conduct performance modeling, endurance optimization, and efficiency enhancement studies.
  • Support research on distributed propulsion and propeller-motor system optimization.
  • Coordinate with super & hypersonics for small turbojet, turbofan, or pulse engines depending on mission needs.
3

Autonomy, Navigation & Control

  • Develop advanced flight control laws, stability augmentation, and fault-tolerant control strategies.
  • Innovate autonomous mission planning, cooperative flight, swarm intelligence, and multi-agent coordination.
  • Integrate DRL-based adaptive navigation, perception-driven obstacle avoidance, and reinforced decision-making models.
  • Work with AI & autonomy specialists to build human-on-the-loop and fully autonomous system variants.
4

Command, Control, Communications & Data Links (C3DL)

  • Design and integrate secure C2 links, telemetry, broadband uplink/downlink channels, and mesh networking.
  • Ensure resilience against jamming, spoofing, EMI/EMC challenges, and contested-spectrum operations.
  • Collaborate with CARNTR, DSPWRC & CCDISR on secure waveform development and protection strategies.
  • Support SATCOM integration and BLOS communication for long-endurance missions.
5

Sensor Payloads, Mission Systems & ISR Capabilities

  • Develop modular payload systems for EO/IR, SAR, hyperspectral, LiDAR, SIGINT, ELINT, mapping & ISR missions.
  • Coordinate with CRMHBF for advanced radar and RF payload integration.
  • Research ultra-light, low-power, miniaturized sensor suites for micro-UAS.
  • Build universal payload interfaces for multi-mission adaptability.
6

Flight Testing, Certification & Safety Frameworks

  • Establish full-spectrum UAV testing infrastructure (ground tests, range tests, endurance trials, wind tunnel inputs).
  • Develop certification pathways for airworthiness, redundancy, safety, and mission-readiness.
  • Maintain data acquisition systems for flight logging, performance analytics, and telemetry processing.
7

Swarm Systems & Multi-Platform Cooperative Operations

  • Create swarming algorithms for distributed sensing, cooperative ISR, and autonomous teaming.
  • Develop inter-UAV coordination schemes, task allocation, and real-time swarm formation control.
  • Research adversarial-resilient swarms under jamming, cyberattack, or degraded GPS environments.
8

Integration of AI, Computer Vision & Onboard Processing

  • Deploy edge-AI architectures using onboard GPU/TPU systems for real-time perception.
  • Innovate onboard threat detection, terrain mapping, collaborative sensing, and target recognition.
  • Cooperate with AI and simulation centres to build digital twins for mission rehearsal.
9

Logistics, Disaster Response & Civil Applications

  • Develop specialized UAVs for precision agriculture, medical delivery, cargo transport, surveying, and emergency relief.
  • Build frameworks for regulatory compliance, BVLOS operations, and safety protocols.
  • Collaborate with national agencies for civil-military dual-use innovation.
10

Prototyping & Technical Workforce Development

  • Establish rapid prototyping labs and composite fabrication facilities.
  • Train specialists in UAV design, flight operations, payload integration, maintenance, and mission planning.
  • Provide technical standards and manufacturing guidelines to national industries and startups.
